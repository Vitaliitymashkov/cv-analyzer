Job Description: senior Project Manager

Position: senior Project Manager
Location: [Your Company Location]
Employment Type: Full-time
Reports To: Project Manager / Program Manager

About the Role

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented senior Project Manager to support our project management team in planning, executing, and delivering projects. This role is ideal for someone with huge experience in their career who is eager to contribute strong organizational, communication, and leadership skills while working on cross-functional initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

Assist Project Managers in coordinating and monitoring project activities.

Support the preparation of project plans, schedules, and progress reports.

Track project deliverables, risks, and issues, escalating as needed.

Maintain project documentation, including meeting minutes, status updates, and task logs.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams (development, QA, operations, business).

Facilitate communication between stakeholders and project team members.

Help organize Agile ceremonies (daily stand-ups, sprint planning, retrospectives).

Requirements

Bachelor’s degree in Business, Management, Computer Science, or related field.

5 years of project coordination or related experience (internship experience acceptable).

Strong organizational and multitasking skills.

Excellent communication (verbal & written) and interpersonal abilities.

Familiarity with project management tools (Jira, Trello, MS Project, Asana).

Knowledge of project management methodologies (Agile, Waterfall, Scrum).

Preferred Qualifications

Relevant certification (e.g., Certified ScrumMaster®, CAPM, or Google Project Management Certificate).

Experience in a tech, consulting, or operations environment.

Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

What We Offer

Opportunity to learn and grow within a supportive project management team.

Exposure to diverse projects across different business areas.

Professional development opportunities, including mentorship and training.

Competitive compensation and benefits package.
